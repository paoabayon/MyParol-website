About Filipino Parols

The Filipino parol (from the Spanish word "farol," meaning lantern) is a traditional Christmas decoration in the Philippines, a star-shaped ornament that represents the Star of Bethlehem.

Originally made from bamboo sticks covered with colored paper, modern parols can be crafted from various materials like coconut leaves, plastic, metal, and even LED lights. During the Christmas season, Filipino homes, streets, and buildings are adorned with these colorful symbols of Filipino Christmas spirit.

At MyParol.com, we're dedicated to bringing this beautiful Filipino tradition to homes around the world, with authentic materials and traditional crafting methods.
